Jiawen Wang

  chiawenw@ucla.edu

“Keep curiosity alive.”

Areas: Embodied AI, Motion Control and Perception, Human-Robot Interaction

I am now a Ph.D. student at the Mechanical Engineering and Aerospace Engineering Department, UCLA, under the supervision of Prof. M. Khalid Jawed. My research interests include motion control, robot learning, and embodied AI.

I got my Master’s degree from Peking University, China, supervised by Prof. Dingsheng Luo. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s degree from NCEPU. Outside of academia, I enjoy playing football (soccer), swimming, and hitting the gym.
